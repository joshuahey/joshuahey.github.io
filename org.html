Mathematical Sciences Society (MSS), '21 - '24

I was the President of the Undergraduate Mathematical and Statistical Sciences Club. Over the past two years, I was also the Social Executive of the club. I spend most of my time hanging out in the club room (CAB 453) socializing, grinding problem sets and playing games.

A few of my responsibilities as the president essentially include overseeing the entire operations of the club, representing the best interests of the club and helping foster a warm and inclusive environment.

We host weekly games night in CAB 457, invite professors to give talks and host competitions. I hosted last year's MSS contest along with Davidson Noby Joseph (ex- President '22) and Muhammad Abul Fazal (ex - Vice President '21) and was the two-time runner-up for the MSS integration Bee.
